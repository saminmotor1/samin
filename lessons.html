Beginner Motorcycle Lessons

(Private, 1 on 1)

Riding a motorcycle is dangerous. You can't ride if you're dead. The majority of accidents happen to new riders within a few weeks because they didn't learn to ride properly. But dont let that scare you because riding is one of the most exhilirating experiences in the world. You will feel truly alive on a bike, experience adrenaline rushing through your veins, and enter into the tightknit community of riders. Learn how to ride safely and you will fall in love with motorcycles.

These lessons are designed for people with ZERO experience with motorcycles. We not only teach you every technique you will need to know about riding, but also ensure that you are going about everything the right way in terms of licensing, your first motorcycle purchase, and proper gear for riding. We will guide you at every step along the way.

How It Works




You show up, learn some basic controls, and within 5 minutes you are on the bike practicing techniques. All lessons are 1 on 1. Just you and the instructor in an empty parking lot. You learn at YOUR pace, not that of someone else. Whether that is fast or slow is up to your learning style, we don't waste anyone's time. You don't need to bring anything because the motorcycle, helmet, and everything you need is provided. Simply arrive on time and be ready to learn.

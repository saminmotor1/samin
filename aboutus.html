About Us




Our Mission

Getting into motorcycles isn't as simple as learning to drive. Our intent is to help people have a smooth and easy transition into riding motorcycles. Many people are intimidated by the idea of riding a motorcycle itself and it certainly doesn't help that there isn't an easy way to learn.

This all started out as teaching some friends in a nearby empty parking lot and that's the atmosphere we strive to preserve. A laid back environment where you feel as if you're being taught by a friend. We have locations in New York and San Diego ultimately hope to expand to every major city across in America.





About the Owner




I grew up in NY and when I was in highschool I knew I wanted to do something different. I decided to move to California and saved up my pennies from my Taco Bell job so I could buy a motorcycle. The whole learning process was an absolute nightmare for me. I just moved to San Diego and didn't know anyone at all, let alone someone that could teach me to ride.

But I didn't let that stop me. I went on Youtube, watched videos, and practiced on the bike I had already bought. I crashed the bike many times, hurt myself, and walked with a limp for a while. I smashed my legs making so many mistakes that could've easily been avoided if I had some guidance. But even though I did it the hard way, I eventually got it.

Now I've been riding on a daily basis for years and its become a big part of what I enjoy doing. So many of my closest friends I met through riding and had I given up, I would have never met these guys. All I want to do is help people get into riding and avoid the struggles I went through.

P.S. If you come through in New York, I'll be the one teaching you.
